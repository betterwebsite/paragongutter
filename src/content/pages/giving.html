Paragon Gutter Supports Agape International Missions

At Paragon Gutter, we believe in using our success to uplift others. That's why we proudly support Agape International Missions (AIM), a nonprofit dedicated to rescuing, healing, and empowering survivors of human trafficking.

  • AIM's Impact

    Since 2014, AIM's SWAT team has rescued over 2,000 individuals from trafficking, leading to the arrest of more than 600 traffickers and resulting in over 1,700 years of prison sentences. Their holistic approach ensures survivors receive the care and support needed to rebuild their lives. Learn more.

  • Paragon's Support

    We are honored to contribute to AIM's mission through financial support and by raising awareness about their life-changing work. Every project we complete helps us continue this support, making a tangible difference in the fight against human trafficking.
